Tastiest Turkey Meatloaf

This is a leaner version of a comfort food classic.

The recipe calls for ground turkey breast which is generally 99% lean, so the addition of plain yogurt is key to maintaining the meatloaf’s unique texture. Ground turkey is also available in 93% lean and can be used in place of the leaner turkey breast.

Recipe adapted from the Betty Crocker website

TOTAL TIME
1 hour 35 mins
YIELD
6

INGREDIENTS
  • 1 ¼ pounds Ground Turkey Breast
  • 6 ounces Greek Fat Free Plain Yogurt
  • ¼ cup ketchup
  • ¾ cup Bread Crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce
  • ¾ teaspoon Salt
  • ¼ teaspoon Ground Sage
  • ¼ teaspoon Pepper
  • 2 cloves Garlic (finely chopped, or 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder)
  • 1 Small Onion (chopped)
  • 1 Egg (slightly beaten)
TOPPING
  • ½ cup ketchup
  • ½ teaspoon Ground Mustard
  • 1 tablespoon Brown Sugar (packed)
INSTRUCTIONS

1. Heat oven to 375°F and gather together the ingredients.

2. In large bowl, mix together all the meatloaf ingredients. Spread mixture evenly into an ungreased 8x4 or 9x5-inch loaf pan, or shape into a standalone 9x5-inch loaf on an ungreased 13x9-inch pan.

3. In small bowl, mix topping ingredients; spread over top (or all the exposed area if making a loaf).

4. Bake uncovered 1 hour to 1 hour 10 minutes or until meat thermometer inserted in center of loaf reads 170°F. Let stand 5 minutes; drain. Remove from pan.
